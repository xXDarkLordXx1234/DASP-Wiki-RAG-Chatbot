---+ Distortion CPE

   * Description
      * Sentence, Token and and TreeTagger are called 2 times, for the _InitialView and for the TranslatedDocument View - done by using the sofaMapping of "TranslatedDocument" to "_InitialView" for the "*OneOfTheAboveAnnotator* 2" Annotators.
      * At the end the DistortionAnnotator writes in the TranslatedView the calculated distortion.
